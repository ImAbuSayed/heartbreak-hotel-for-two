[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I left the porch light burning by the open door
Craving the way your shadows dance upon my floor
There's a midnight room with nothing left to do
Let's check into this heartbreak hotel for two

[Verse 1]
The rain is sliding down the dusty window pane
Washing the world away so only we remain
I pour a little whiskey, watch the embers glow
And feel the heavy tension start to build and grow

[Pre-Chorus]
The silence whispers things we never say out loud
Far from the heavy rushing of the endless crowd
I feel the distance closing in the quiet night
Just waiting for your touch to make the feeling right

[Chorus]
Oh, we built this sanctuary out of rust and wood
A secret hideaway where everything is understood
It's just me and you against the bitter cold
In this heartbreak hotel where our bodies fold

[Post-Chorus]
(Where our bodies fold)
Yeah, a heartbreak hotel for two

[Verse 2]
I hear your footsteps slow upon the wooden stairs
Running my heavy fingers through your messy hair
The scent of you is drowning out the sudden storm
A soft and tender fire keeping both of us warm

[Pre-Chorus 2]

A slow and steady heartbeat in the quiet gloom

[Chorus]
